The first book of Alchemy Reigns! is now complete. Set in a world where tension and fear run high due to the presence of humans with the ability to conjure elemental forces at will.

Book 1 is titled Breaking Silence, and follows the journeys of Artisa, a member of the Tech regime, and Veston, a human boy.

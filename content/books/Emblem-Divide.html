Blurb

The lecturer began commenting. “Oken Brown. He looks harmless enough but he was one of the most ruthless criminals ever to have graced God’s earth.” Suddenly Zane became interested. “He developed the first hand held temporal weapon, and used it in an underground fighting arena only twenty years ago.”

It wasn’t as if Oken hadn’t killed before, he was an elite member of an underground culture. He was almost like an ambassador of death. Oken was one of the best, but even he shuddered at this new weapon which threatened the very existence of everyone on the planet.

About the book

Emblem Divide was released on a wordpress blog in the winter of 2009. It was released serially, as a chapter every two days. Emblem Divide was released for free and readers were encouraged to donate money to three charities.

The book was started in 2003 whilst I was studying at university in a little under a month I had written almost 40,000 words. Quite frankly the most I had ever written in my life. The book then took a back seat for 6 years. My wife who had read the beginnings of it, begged me to finish it and 6 years later I did and gave it to her as a 5 year wedding anniversary present.

Shortly afterwards, I asked her if she would mind me relseasing the book online for free as an attempt to raise money for charity. The seed was sown and emblemdivide.com was set up on a wordpress blog.
